# Customer Stories: How Microbiome Testing Changed My Health Understanding gut health and the composition of the gut microbiome is increasingly relevant to personalized health decisions. This review synthesizes real customer experiences and evidence-based context around the InnerBuddies microbiome test kit. For background information and purchasing details, see the [InnerBuddies microbiome test kit review](https://www.innerbuddies.com/blogs/gut-health/innerbuddies-microbiome-test-kit-review-real-customer-stories-gut-health-benefits). ## What the test does and what it measures Microbiome testing profiles the types and relative abundances of bacteria (and sometimes other microbes) in stool samples. The InnerBuddies kit collects a small sample for laboratory sequencing and analysis. Results typically report measures such as species richness and the presence of bacterial groups associated with digestion, inflammation, or metabolic processes. In clinical and research settings, such profiling can help generate hypotheses about diet, symptom patterns, and interventions, though it is not a standalone diagnostic tool. For a technical perspective on microbiome testing used in gastrointestinal conditions, readers may consult expert summaries such as Gut Microbiome Testing for IBS: Personalized Diagnosis & Intervention. ## Report format and usability Customers report that the kit includes step-by-step sampling instructions and a user-friendly report. The analysis usually translates sequencing data into practical categories (e.g., fiber-degrading bacteria, potential opportunistic species) and pairs those with general lifestyle or dietary suggestions. While actionable guidance can be helpful, clinicians advise interpreting these suggestions alongside clinical history and laboratory markers. ## Real customer experiences and outcomes A number of users describe improvements in symptoms after applying personalized recommendations based on their microbiome profiles. One user case noted weight-management support after altering macronutrient composition informed by their microbial profile. It is important to emphasize that individual responses vary.
